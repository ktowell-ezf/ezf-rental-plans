Changelog

A record of updates to the wireframe across versions.

v2 Version 2

June 23, 2026
  • 1

    Rental booking: Details tab updated to show the full rental details form, including venue, instructor (multiselect), rental type, title, dates, times, recurrence, and notes — pre-filled with demo data.

  • 2

    Rental booking: Client Selection (new step 2.2) updated to show the typical client selection process — a searchable grid of clients with a single-select radio pattern. Matches the existing EZFacility client picker UI.

  • 3

    Rental booking: Billing (step 2.3) updated with revised plans-vs-rates filtering and expanded plan options.

    Select Rental Special to see the apply-to options: This occurrence, Remaining occurrences (with tooltip), and Custom. Choose Custom to explore the session-picker grid, including the error state when selected occurrences exceed the plan balance.
  • 4

    Rental booking: Confirmation (step 2.4) now supports adding an additional client for billing on the same rental. A new Booking: Confirmation (Multiple clients) step shows the updated Client(s) Billed grid with both clients listed.

  • 5

    General tidying up — navigation step names updated, panel widths adjusted for readability, client profile summary bar placeholder added, and minor layout consistency fixes throughout.
